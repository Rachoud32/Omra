Plan Details

Embark on an unconventional plan to fully relish the experience.

According to your international flight schedule, we will be waiting for you in Jeddah airport. Transfer to your hotel in Makkah. After check-in and have a quick shower, your local guide will meet you in hotel lobby and walk together to the Haram-Masjeed al Haram to perform your first Umrah. Once you finish the Umrah, you can either return to hotel or stay in Haram on your own. Rest of the day at your pace. Overnight in Makkah.

Breakfast at the hotel and early departure from your hotel for Ziyarats in Makkah with your English speaking driver. Today you will visit Maqbarat el Ma'la(Cemetery) resting place of Hazrat Khadijah bint Khuwaylid, first wife of our beloved Nabi(Saw), His first Son and many other Sahabis. The visit will be outside the fences from a distance. Continue to Arafat to feel the atmosphere of Hajj where the millions gather in Hajj season for waqfa and du'a (If you want to perform Umrah once more, you can get to Ikhram here as the Arafat also a Miqat -Haram border-point to enter Ikhram). Muzdalifah, Jamarat etc. We will return to our hotel before noon prayer. Rest of the day at your own pace. Overnight in Makkah.

Breakfast at the hotel and you can make your ibadah at your heart's content. If you want, you can visit Noor Mountain where cave of Hira is located on your own. Overnight in Makkah.

Breakfast at the hotel and you can make your ibadah at your heart's content. If you want to visit Taif city, then we can arrange a day trip with additional cost. Overnight in Makkah.

Breakfast at the hotel and you can make your ibadah at your heart's content. If you would like, we can also arrange an additional Al Ji'ranah Umrah where Nabi(saw) entered Ikhram to perform Umrah after battle of Hunayn. Overnight in Makkah.
